cntr-opt-n --- new text file

opt-command-esc --- force quit app
opt-Number - iTerm, choose a split panel

opt+f --- open finder
opt+z --- open zed
opt+t --- open iTerm
opt+s --- open Sourcetree

cntr+E – Moves the cursor to the end of the line.
